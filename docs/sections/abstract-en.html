Abstract

Transmission Experiment Ontology is developed by SummerFair project (SUMmarizing antiMicrobial transmission data to Enable data Reanalysis and predictions by FAIR data use). The lack of a common terminology in the AMR transmission experiments leads to repetition of costly experiments. By developing a domain ontology we can reuse and combine the AMR data, which leads to the improvement of an estimation of transmission parameters and prediction of outcomes of yet unobserved situations.
